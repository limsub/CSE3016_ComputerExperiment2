- **Half Adder**
    - $$S = \overline{A}B + A\overline{B} =  A⊕B$$
    - $$C = AB$$


<br>


- **Full Adder**
    - $$ S = (A⊕B)⊕C_{in} $$
    - $$ C_{out} = C_{in} (A⊕B) + AB

- **Half Subtractor**
    - $$ D = \overline{A}B + A\overline{B} = A⊕B$$
    - $$ b = \overline{A}B $$

    - $$ D_n = A_n ⊕ B_n ⊕ b_{n-1}$$
